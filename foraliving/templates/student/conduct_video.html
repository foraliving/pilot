    Tips for conducting your interview

    1. Ask interviewee one of your questions and then listen as they answer. Take notes and ask follow-up questions on things you found interesting or didn’t understand.

    2. Don’t forget to thank your interviewee and let me them what you learned or what interested you about their story after the interview is complete!

    About the assignment

    1. Each question will be a new video.

    2. Practice the recording process with a parnter so that you will be ready for the real interview.

    3. Be sure that the device or devices you are usings are fully charged. If you are using a computer, plug it in!

    4. You can start the recording over if something goes wrong.

    5. If connecting to the person online, they will not be able to see you but will hear your voice.

    6. If you are interviewing someone in person, you and your partner will put one computer facing the interviewee and the other one facing you.

    • The interviewee will log into For A Living on the computer that will record them.
    • You and your partner will check the settings and control the recording on your computer.
